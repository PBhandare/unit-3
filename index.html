Labor Certification Application Viewer for 2025Q1 Submissions

The Labor Certification Application is one of the steps that is a part of the H-1B filing petition package. It is submitted to the Depertment of Labor as a part of showing the applicant will not be taking a job from an American and is participating in labor practices commesurate with standards for the industry and occupation.

Standard Occupational Classification Code Entropy

The map currently displayed shows how dominant or evenly distributed states are by their Standard Occupational Classification (SOC) codes. Lower entropy means that there is more singular dominant categories and high entropy means that there is more diversity.
